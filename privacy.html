Branch Off Holdings privacy notice

This page explains how Branch Off Holdings handles information submitted through the inquiry form on this website.

What we collect

We may collect the information you submit directly, including your name, email address, company name, phone number, inquiry category, and message content.

How we use it

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Review partnership, licensing, and service requests
  • Route submissions into controlled workflow systems
  • Operate and improve the inquiry process

Data handling

Submitted inquiries may be stored in BranchOps-controlled systems and may also be mirrored to operational tools used for follow-up and workflow management.

Contact

If you have questions about this notice or your submitted information, contact admin@branchoffholdings.com.
