Founder Note

Why this holdings structure exists.

The point of Branch Off Holdings is to keep control where it belongs. Systems, brands, and infrastructure should become owned assets that can compound over time instead of being rented from weak platforms or trapped in ad hoc operations.

Branch Off Holdings is built to support long-horizon ownership, cleaner licensing, and operating infrastructure that can be transferred, expanded, and protected. The emphasis is not speed for its own sake. The emphasis is control, repeatability, and asset value.
